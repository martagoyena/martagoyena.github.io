About me

Born in Pamplona/Iruña, Navarra, Spain. I am a researcher at Nokia Transformation, Engineering & Consulting Services Spain (TECSS) since March 2024, working in the XR Lab on the Incluverso 5G project. I am also a PhD student at the Grupo de Tratamiento de Imágenes (GTI) of the Universidad Politécnica de Madrid (UPM).

I hold a Bachelor's degree in Telecommunication Engineering from the Universidad Pública de Navarra (UPNA) and a Master's degree in Telecommunication Engineering from the Universidad Politécnica de Madrid (UPM). During my studies, I completed an Erasmus stay at the École Supérieure d'Ingénieurs en Électronique et Électrotechnique (ESIEE) in Paris, where I developed a brain-computer interface system for prosthesis control. My master's thesis involved creating an immersive therapy tool for people with stair phobia and intellectual disabilities.

My research interests include immersive and accessible XR technologies, cognitive and behavioral therapy, and inclusive human-computer interaction.
